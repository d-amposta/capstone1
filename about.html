About the Site

Boyd's Anatomy teaches anatomy at the comfort of your browser. It delivers a clean interface so the user can focus on the content. The site aims to help students understand anatomy through the use of simple explanations and pictures curated by some of the world's best medical professionals.

About the Author

The author is a Physical Therapy graduate from De La Salle - Health Sciences Institute in Dasmarinas, Cavite. He has worked as a Physical Therapist in a hospital in Cavite for 3 years and has previously taught physical therapy students in his alma mater for 1 year.
